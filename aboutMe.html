About me

Quick introduction about me:
Half Spanish half Swedish student at KTH entering my third year at ICT bachelor's program at KTH. Service minded, quick learner, analytic and curious at the same time.

Born and raised in Spain, in a little town called Pamplona, I decided to move to Stockholm to start my university studies. Since an early age has science fascinated me, especially programming, maths and logic. They unveil unlimited possibilities and can even become fun when you achieve to understand what they try to communicate. Not only that, but it can obviously be applied to a wide variety of situations, from helping people in a hospital to communicating with astronauts in outer space. These are two of the main reasons why I chose to study IT: service and reachability.
